How to make Banana Bread

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all- purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 beaten eggs
  • 2 1/3 cups mashed overripe bananas

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F 175 degrees C. Lightly grease a 9*5 inch loaf pan.
  2. In a large ball, combine flour, baking soda and salt. In seperate bowl, cream together butter and brown sugar.
  3. Stir in eggs and mashed bananas until well blended. Stir banana mixture into flour mixture, stir just to moisten.
  4. Pour butter into prepared loaf pan
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 60 to 65 minutes, until a toothpich inserted into center of the loaf comes out clean.
  6. Let bread cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack.
